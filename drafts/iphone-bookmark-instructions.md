# iPhone Home Screen Bookmark Instructions

## Safari (iPhone/iPad)
1. Open Safari and navigate to your DMAI purl URL
2. Tap the **Share button** (square with arrow pointing up) at the bottom
3. Scroll down and select **"Add to Home Screen"**
4. Edit the name if desired (it will show under the icon)
5. Tap **"Add"** in the top right
6. The icon will appear on your home screen like a regular app

## Chrome (iPhone/iPad) 
1. Open Chrome and navigate to your DMAI purl URL
2. Tap the **three dots menu** (⋯) in the bottom right
3. Select **"Add to Home Screen"**
4. Edit the name if desired
5. Tap **"Add"**
6. The icon will appear on your home screen

## What This Creates
- A clickable icon on your iPhone/iPad home screen
- Works like a regular app - one tap opens your purl in the browser
- Stays on your home screen permanently until manually removed
- The technical name for this is a "Progressive Web App" (PWA) shortcut
