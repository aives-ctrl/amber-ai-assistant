# MINDFIRE.md - Company & Product Knowledge

## Core Value Proposition
**"Physical Delivery. Digital Intelligence."**

MindFire turns paper into intelligent, interactive experiences through AI-powered QR codes that enable natural conversations.

## The Technology: AI-Powered Print

### How It Works
1. **PQR (Personalized QR Code)** - Special QR codes on any printed material
2. **Scan & Talk** - Recipients scan with phone, connect to AI voice agent
3. **Natural Conversation** - AI answers questions, provides information, schedules appointments
4. **Multi-Language Support** - English, Spanish, Portuguese, French, others
5. **Complete Tracking** - Every interaction measured from initial scan to conversion

### What Makes It Powerful
- **Always Available**: 24/7/365 AI agent, no holidays or time zones
- **Perfectly Consistent**: Same quality conversation every time
- **Constantly Learning**: Gets smarter from every interaction
- **Infinitely Scalable**: Handles one conversation or thousands simultaneously
- **Cross-Channel Connected**: Start in mail, continue via text/email
- **Real-Time Lead Qualification**: System evaluates interest levels
- **Complete Attribution**: Track from mail delivery to final sale

## Key Messages & Positioning

- Customers have questions but no way to get immediate answers
- No measurement or attribution for print campaigns
- Call centers overwhelmed with basic questions

### Our Solution
- Make paper intelligent and conversational
- Instant answers through natural AI conversation
- Complete measurement from print to revenue
- Scalable customer engagement

### Competitive Advantages
