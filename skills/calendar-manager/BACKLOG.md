- **Follow-up scheduling:** Coordinate next steps from completed meetings
- **Multi-timezone coordination:** Handle scheduling across time zones for remote participants

- **Calendar sharing:** Optimized availability sharing with external contacts

- **Integration with other systems:** Sync with CRM, project management tools

- **Calendar backup/recovery:** Historical meeting data preservation
