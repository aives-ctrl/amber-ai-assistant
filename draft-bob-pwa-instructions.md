# PWA Instructions for Bob & Team

Hey Bob (and Dave),

That "little icon thingy" is called a PWA (Progressive Web App) or "Add to Home Screen" bookmark. Here are the steps:

## iPhone/iPad (Safari):
1. Open the website in Safari
2. Tap the share button (square with arrow pointing up)
3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
4. Edit the name if needed, then tap "Add"
5. The icon will appear on your home screen like a regular app

## iPhone/iPad (Chrome):
1. Open the website in Chrome
2. Tap the three dots menu (⋯) 
3. Tap "Add to Home Screen"
4. Edit the name if needed, then tap "Add"

## Android (Chrome):
1. Open the website in Chrome
2. Tap the three dots menu
3. Tap "Add to Home Screen" or "Install app" (if available)
4. Confirm the installation

The website needs to be PWA-enabled for this to work properly (which most modern business sites are). Once added, it'll open in a browser-like app experience without the typical browser navigation bars.

Let me know if anyone hits snags with this process!
