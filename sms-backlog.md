# SMS Channel Limitations & Backlog

## Current Limitations
- **No image support** - Images sent via SMS don't come through to OpenClaw
  - Date identified: March 2, 2026
  - Impact: Can't share screenshots, photos, or visual info through SMS
  - Workaround: Use other channels (Telegram, email) for image sharing
